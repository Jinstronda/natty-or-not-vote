# Bulk Expert Review Import Format

Each line should be:
expert_name,influencer_name,natty_or_not,comment,url

- expert_name: Name of the expert (must match or will be created)
- influencer_name: Name of the influencer (must match or will be created)
- natty_or_not: "natty" or "juicy"
- comment: The expert's review comment
- url: (optional) Link to the full review

Example:
Dr. Smith,John Doe,natty,Looks natural based on muscle insertions.,https://example.com/review1
Dr. Smith,Jane Fit,juicy,Obvious signs of enhancement.,
